Overview

TEALEAVES is a tea company that specializes in the highest quality loose leaf and bagged tea. They cater to five-star properties and notable culinary leaders worldwide, as well as people who want to enjoy tea in the comfort of their own home; this is done with the goal in mind to inspire connections and creativity while on the search for the ultimate lifestyle. In addition to being known as a leading luxury tea blender, their strengths also lie in their ability to coordinate collaborations with prominent brands, such as Pantone, RUDD, and Microsoft, and their ability to create remarkable assets in film, photography, copy, articles, and social media pieces. TEALEAVES’ core values revolve around craftsmanship, innovation, art and beauty and the company holds great importance on detail-orientation, constant innovation, and shaping trends.


Some of the exciting projects that I’ve had the opportunity to work on:

  • a motion graphic that was part of the Language of Aroma panel presentation at SXSW 2018

  • the packaging label design of their new loose leaf retail tins that include updated and iconic watercolours

  • building the design of the new Shopify webstore, largely evident on the homepage and product pages
