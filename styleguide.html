Fonts:

Canela

Used for the accent text, project descriptions, and captions.

Canela is a graceful display typeface that defies many of the traditional classifications. Designed by Miguel Reyes, its forms are in an ambiguous space between sans and serif, both soft and sharp, modern yet with roots in the classical. — Commercial Type

Nimbus Sans

Used for body text.

Nimbus Sans is a sans-serif typeface published by Germany-based URW++. The design is based on Helvetica but attempts to clean up some of the “mistakes” of the original Helvetica. Nimbus Sans is available in a huge variety of cuts, including condensed and extended versions. — Typewolf

Nimbus Sans Extended

Used for the bold project names and text headings.
